The only way to stop pollution on the land and in the oceans is to prevent getting any unnecessary waste on the ground and into water.

While pollution is on the ground, the further development of the process is dissolving and getting into water streams of rivers and then to the seas and oceans. Dissolving waste takes hundreds of years. Therefore, we can't wait and need to act now. We can stop the dumping the waste and we can collect those that already on the ground by our own efforts.
